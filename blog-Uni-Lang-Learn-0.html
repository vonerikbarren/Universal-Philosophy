Hello Everyone!

Chinese being my 10th language technically, was probably the hardest language I had to learn. It was a language that challenged me and gave me new mental heights to strive for. I have wanted to learn Chinese since I learned Kungfu from when I was just a child. When I finally got to Chinese it was so difficult I considered retaking it for more experience on the basics. Despite its difficulty, with consistency and alot of resources, anyone can speak this beautiful language on their own.
